Manifest

Principles worth pinning

A strong vision board is not only aesthetics. It also states what kind of work this future will and will not accept.

Make the page feel like a place

Visitors should sense a room, a climate, and a point of view. That feeling is part of the product.

Build from signal, not noise

Not every idea deserves a feature. The best additions intensify the emotional center instead of distracting from it.

Leave room for becoming

The board should not trap the vision. It should give it shape while allowing the project to reveal more of itself.
